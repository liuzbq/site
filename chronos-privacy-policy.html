Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Chronos collects, uses, stores, and protects your information when you use the app. Last updated: April 2026.

1. Information We Collect

Chronos may collect account details such as your email address or Apple sign-in identity, capsule titles, unlock times, written notes, voice recordings, uploaded images, AI-generated results, subscription status, notification settings, and limited technical information needed to operate the service.

2. How We Use Information

We use your information to provide core app features, sync your capsules across sessions, store uploaded media, support capsule unlock notifications, restore purchases, improve reliability, and run optional AI analysis features for eligible accounts.

3. Storage And Processing

Chronos uses third-party infrastructure to operate the service, including Supabase for authentication, database, storage, and server functions, OneSignal for push notifications, Apple for in-app purchases, and AI-related providers when analysis features are enabled.

4. AI Features

If AI-related features are enabled for your account or plan, capsule data may be processed by server-side AI services during seal and open flows. AI-generated output is intended to support reflection features and may not always be complete or accurate.

5. Notifications

If you allow notifications on your device, Chronos may send push reminders such as capsule unlock notifications. If notifications are disabled in system settings, those reminders may stop. Notification status may be synchronized to help avoid unnecessary delivery attempts.

6. Local Device Data

Chronos may temporarily store downloaded media on your device to improve playback speed and reduce repeated downloads. Clearing local cache removes those local copies only and does not delete your cloud-stored capsule content.

7. Data Retention

Your account and capsule data are retained while your account remains active unless removed through future account deletion tools, support assistance, or legal obligations. Cached local media may remain on-device until cleared manually or removed by the operating system.

8. Your Choices

You may manage notification permissions in device settings, clear local cache within the app, control whether automatic AI analysis is enabled, and manage subscription status through Apple purchase tools. Additional data controls may be added over time.

9. Children

Chronos is not intended for use by children without appropriate supervision where required by applicable law.
